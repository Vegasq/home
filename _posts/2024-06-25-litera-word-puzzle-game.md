## What is Litera?

Litera is a word puzzle game that challenges players with Scrabble-style gameplay. It's designed to be both engaging and intellectually stimulating, perfect for word game enthusiasts who want to test their vocabulary and strategic thinking.

## Features

- Classic Scrabble-style word building mechanics
- Challenging puzzles that test your vocabulary
- Clean, intuitive interface designed for iOS
- Perfect for quick gaming sessions or longer puzzle-solving marathons
