## What is DinoDB?

DinoDB is a native macOS application that brings an intuitive (questionably), familiar interface to DynamoDB management.


## Try It Out

DinoDB is available now for macOS. Whether you're managing a single table or juggling multiple AWS accounts, give it a try.
